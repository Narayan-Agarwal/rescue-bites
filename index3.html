Donate food

This project aims to ensure the hunger ratio around the globe to reduce to 0 i.e. ensuring that no one sleeps hungry and gets proper fooding through-out their life. It aims to deliver food to those remote regions of the globe who are deprived of food almost every time and every day. It aims to end hunger, achieve food security and quality and improved nutrition with a promotion to sustainable agriculture.
